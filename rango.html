4. Rango




(wikipedia)

Rango is a main character of animated movie Rango. He is a intelligent and eccentric chameleon who is initially a pet of a human family but afterwards an incident with the car he as falls out of his box and the car having himself finally free and he arrives in the Western city called Dirt where he becomes the new sheriff and start to becoming a great hero.Although "Rango" is not his real name, he got the name idea from a beverage in the town of Dirt, which the townsfolk believe is his actual name.
