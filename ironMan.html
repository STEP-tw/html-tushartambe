3. Iron Man




(wikipedia)

Iron Man is a fictional superhero in Marvel cinematic universe. It is performed by the Robert Downey Jr.It is the most loved superhero character. He is great mechanic. He can create what he want and that makes him appealing.He is arrogant.Iron man is among the super heroes who have a good sense of humor. A typical iron man movie usually has parts that make people laugh. Tony stark's personality is very complex. He is a business man and CEO but he is also a super hero and a great engineer. The complexity of his personality makes him more appealing to many people.
